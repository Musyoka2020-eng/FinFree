What is a Conscious Spending Plan?

Unlike traditional budgets that restrict everything, a Conscious Spending Plan helps you spend money on things you love while automatically saving and cutting costs on things you don't care about.

  • Spend on what you love
    Identify your values and spend generously on what matters most to you
  • Cut costs mercilessly
    Eliminate spending on things that don't align with your values
  • Automate everything
    Set up systems so your money flows automatically to the right places
